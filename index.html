About Growell

Your Trusted Partner for Medical Education in China and Other Country

Growell Educational Institute specializes in helping international students pursue medical degrees in China's prestigious universities. With over 10 years of experience, we've guided hundreds of students through the complex process of admission, visa application, and cultural adaptation.

Our team consists of former international students and education experts who understand the challenges you may face and are dedicated to making your journey smooth and successful.

We maintain strong relationships with top medical universities across China, ensuring our students receive the best possible education and support throughout their academic journey.

Our Services

University Admission Assistance

Comprehensive support with selecting the right university, preparing application documents, and securing admission to China's top medical schools.

Visa Application Support

Expert guidance through the entire visa application process, including document preparation, interview coaching, and follow-up with embassies.

Pre-Departure Orientation

Essential training on Chinese culture, academic expectations, and practical living tips to help you adapt quickly to your new environment.

Ongoing Student Support

24/7 assistance with academic challenges, accommodation issues, and any other concerns you may have during your studies in China.

Partner Universities

We work with China's top-ranked medical universities that are recognized by WHO, MCI, and other international medical councils.
